About Us

Frontend Simplified is a modern web development learning platform dedicated to helping aspiring developers build real-world, job-ready skills. Through structured courses, practical projects, and clear, beginner-friendly explanations, it focuses on teaching the technologies that power today's web, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, and related development tools. Its mission is to simplify complex frontend concepts, bridge the gap between theory and professional practice, and prepare students to confidently pursue careers as frontend developers.
